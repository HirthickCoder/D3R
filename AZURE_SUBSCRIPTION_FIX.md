# Azure Subscription Activation Issue - Solution Guide

## üö® Problem Detected

Your Azure Portal shows: **"No subscriptions in student.tce.edu"**

This means your Azure for Students subscription is either:
1. Not yet activated
2. Not linked to your current directory
3. Expired or pending verification

## ‚úÖ Solution Steps

### Option 1: Activate Azure for Students (If Not Done)

1. **Go to Azure Education Hub**:
   - Visit: https://aka.ms/azure4students
   - Or: https://portal.azure.com/#blade/Microsoft_Azure_Education/EducationMenuBlade/overview

2. **Sign in** with your student email: `hirthick@student.tce.edu`

3. **Verify your student status**:
   - You may need to verify with your school email
   - Or upload proof of enrollment
   - Or use your school's verification system

4. **Activate the subscription**:
   - Follow the prompts to activate Azure for Students
   - You should get $100 credit

5. **Wait 5-10 minutes** for activation to complete

6. **Refresh Azure Portal** and check if subscription appears

### Option 2: Check Existing Subscription Status

1. **Go to Azure Education Hub**:
   - Visit: https://portal.azure.com/#blade/Microsoft_Azure_Education/EducationMenuBlade/overview

2. **Check your subscription status**:
   - Look for "Azure for Students" subscription
   - Check if it shows as "Active"
   - Check expiration date

3. **If subscription exists but not visible**:
   - Try switching directories in Azure Portal
   - Click your profile icon (top right)
   - Click "Switch directory"
   - Look for a directory that shows subscriptions

### Option 3: Use Azure Portal Direct Links

Try these direct links to activate/check your subscription:

1. **Azure for Students Sign-up**:
   ```
   https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/students/
   ```

2. **Azure Education Hub**:
   ```
   https://portal.azure.com/#blade/Microsoft_Azure_Education/EducationMenuBlade/overview
   ```

3. **Subscriptions Page**:
   ```
   https://portal.azure.com/#view/Microsoft_Azure_Billing/SubscriptionsBlade
   ```

### Option 4: Contact Azure Support

If none of the above work:

1. **Azure Education Support**:
   - Email: azureforeducation@microsoft.com
   - Include your student email and school name

2. **Azure Portal Support**:
   - Click "?" icon in Azure Portal (top right)
   - Click "Help + support"
   - Create a support request

## üîç How to Verify Subscription is Active

After activation, verify by:

1. Go to: https://portal.azure.com/#view/Microsoft_Azure_Billing/SubscriptionsBlade
2. You should see "Azure for Students" subscription
3. Status should be "Active"
4. You should see your remaining credit (up to $100)

## üìù What to Do After Activation

Once your subscription is active:

1. **Return to Azure Portal home**: https://portal.azure.com
2. **Follow the deployment guide**: Open `DEPLOY_GUIDE.md` in your project
3. **Create resources**:
   - Static Web App for frontend
   - App Service for backend
